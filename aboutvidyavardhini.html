Aim's & Objectives of Vidyavardhini

1. To provide affordable quality College Education to all students of Vasai and adjoining areas, right here in Vasai.
2. To actively promote the noble cause of higher education for girls.
3. To ensure that not a single deserving student of this region is deprived of College Education merely because of adverse social and economical factors.
4. To instill in our students a strong feeling of self-confidence So that they become adequately equipped to compete favorably with students from Mumbai in all spheres be it job opportunities or further education or whatsoever.
5. To create amongst students, social awareness regarding various forms of inequalities arising from social and economic factors, and to imbibe in them a spirit to fight injustice at all levels.
