Aim's & Objectives of Vidyavardhini

1. To provide affordable quality College Education to all students of Vasai and adjoining areas, right here in Vasai.
2. To actively promote the noble cause of higher education for girls.
3. To ensure that not a single deserving student of this region is deprived of College Education merely because of adverse social and economical factors.
4. To instill in our students a strong feeling of self-confidence So that they become adequately equipped to compete favorably with students from Mumbai in all spheres be it job opportunities or further education or whatsoever.
5. To create amongst students, social awareness regarding various forms of inequalities arising from social and economic factors, and to imbibe in them a spirit to fight injustice at all levels.

The late Padmashri Bhausaheb Vartak was a visionary par excellence and a devoted social worker. He founded the Vidyavardhini in 1970 as an institution to provide an opportunity to all students in the vicinity of Vasai an affordable quality College Education which was the need of the hour. Today Vidyavardhini has grown into an institute of great repute with the most reputed and famous Junior College with the streams of Arts (Both Marathi and English Medium),Science, Commerce, M.C.V.C (All English medium) and three Degree Colleges, Engineering Degree College and B. V. Polytechnic offering Diplomas in various branches of Engineering.

Under the able guidance of the Ex President of Vidyavardhini Late Smt. Taramai Vartak and Late Shri. Pratapbhai Khokhani, Vidyavardhini has taken big strides and carved for itself a niche in Maharashtra as one of the best educational institutions. The aim of the institution is to provide not only quality education but a holistic development of personality of the student and make him a man of good culture. It encourages students to excel not only in academics but also in co-curricular activities and thus making them fully rounded personalities able to face the challenges of the world. Located just a short distance away from Vasai Road Station, Vidyavardhini’s Colleges are nestled in the midst of a sprawling campus.
